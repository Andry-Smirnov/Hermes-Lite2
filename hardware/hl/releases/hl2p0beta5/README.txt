This directory contains all files necessary to fabricate the Hermes-Lite 2.0beta5 release. Elecrow is the preferred provider as they have and are developing expertise in the Hermes-Lite 2.0.

Only the gerber files in the .zip package are required if just printed circuit boards are desired. Also, PCBs can be ordered directly online without corresponding with Jenny or Helen.
